Hi
I’m Mario Ranftl

I’m an information manager and hold a master’s (2014) and bachelor’s (2011) degree from FH JOANNEUM Austria (information management degree programme). My studies helped me become an IT generalist and covered both technical (e.g. network infrastructure & security, application development, enterprise IT architecture etc.) and business related fields (project, process, change MGMT etc.). This site is an experiment to write (and ramble on) about my professional life.

I’m currently out of work, but expecting to get started full-time in the near future (anywhere! go ahead and catch my interest). In the meantime, I’m very much into open source projects, contribute here and there and working on some small projects (e.g. SJgrabber). I love well documented, tested and maintainable code bases. My preferred programming language at the moment is JavaScript, but I’ve also had a hell of a time with AS3/Flex, C, C# and Java (and unfortunately VB).

Beware, I’m not a typical frontend dev by any means. Abstracting and optimizing lies in my very nature! Data modeling, code decoupling, applying design patterns, tooling, testing and deploying is what I do and love.

Apart from my professional life, I have a weakness for weird / experimental music (I’m a big Mike Patton and Trent Reznor fan), hot ‘n’ spicy food, beautiful beaches and indie / retro (SNES style) games. Furthermore I play the guitar (mediocre) and the piano (poorly) and skim through tekkie / startup related articles everyday.

TL;DR

information manager, DI (MSc equiv.) & BSc, full stack engineer, wannabe entrepreneur, mediocre guitar player, above average nerd
