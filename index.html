Hi!

I’m Mario Ranftl.

A.k.a. majodev & ranf.tl on The Internets.

I’m an Austrian IT professional who holds a master’s and bachelor’s degree in information management from FH JOANNEUM Graz, Austria. I speak both IT[1] and business[2] and currently live in Fehring (Styria, Austria).

This site is an experiment to write and ramble on about my professional life and open source software.

I’m currently out of work, but expecting to get started full-time in the near future[3]. In the meantime, I’m very much into open source projects, contribute here and there and work on some small projects. I love well documented, tested and maintainable code. At the very moment, my language of choice is JavaScript, but I’ve also had a hell of a time with AS3/Flex, PHP and Java (and unfortunately VB).

Beware, I’m not a creative designer[4], however I really like to work with these people. I am a reliable team player with excellent communication skills. Abstracting and optimizing lies in my very nature. Data modeling, code decoupling, applying design patterns, tooling, testing and deploying is what I do and love.

Apart from my professional life, I have a weakness for weird music[5], hot ‘n’ spicy food, beautiful beaches and indie or retro SNES games. Furthermore I play the guitar (poorly) and the piano (also poorly) and skim through tekkie / startup related articles everyday.
